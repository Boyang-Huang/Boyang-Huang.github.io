Boyang Huang

Hi! I am a second-year master's student in the Computer Science & Engineering department at the University of California San Diego, where I'm very fortunate to work with Professor Russell Impagliazzo and Barna Saha. My research interest is in computational complexity theory and algorithm design, but I'm also braodly interested in theoretical computer science and mathematics. Prior to UCSD, I received undergraduate degrees in computer science and honors mathematics from the University of Michigan Ann Arbor.

I am deeply thankful to all my research advisors, mentors, and collaborators for guiding me into theory research. I am also extremely grateful to all my previous professors and instructors for introducing me to the beautiful world of mathematics.
